§ About the troop

What we're about.

Scouts BSA Troop 12 has been part of Anytown since 1962. We're a Scout-led troop of roughly 32 youth and 18 trained adult leaders. We camp once a month, run a High Adventure trek every summer (rotating Philmont, Sea Base, Boundary Waters, and the Jamboree), and serve our community through projects with local food shelves, the fire department's Fire & Ice festival, and the Lions Club. New scouts and visiting families are always welcome at our Tuesday meetings — bring questions, leave with a calendar.
