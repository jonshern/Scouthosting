§ What we don't do

Scoutbook handles ranks. Compass handles everything else.

Scouting America already has Scoutbook for ranks, merit badges, partials, and awards. Compass doesn't try to compete with it — we deep-link from every Scout profile so leaders aren't entering the same data twice.

What we focus on instead is the stuff Scoutbook doesn't cover — the parent group-text, the campout RSVP, the potluck sign-up, the Sunday-evening cancellation, the weekly newsletter. The communication layer that makes a unit feel run rather than rumored. Troops who communicate well, succeed.
