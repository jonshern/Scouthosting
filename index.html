Everything your unit needs

One platform. All the pieces.

Stop stitching together five tools. Compass bundles every feature a unit needs in one clean, modern interface.

Calendar & events

Drag‑and‑drop events, RSVP and sign‑up sheets, recurring meetings, ICS export, automatic reminders. One‑click "Add to Google Calendar" on every event.

Directions to every event

Each event auto-generates Google Maps, Apple Maps, and Waze links from its location, plus a carpool sign‑up so families can share the drive.

Trip & meal planner

Headcount, meal plan, dietary flags, and an auto‑generated shopping list. Recipes scale to your real attendance and reconcile to the event fee.

Member directory

Family-aware roster with patrols, leadership positions, contact info, and per‑member privacy controls.

Scoutbook deep-links

Scoutbook is the official advancement system — we don't duplicate it. We deep-link from every Scout profile and surface read-only summaries (when BSA's API permits).

Group email

Send to the whole troop, a patrol, or a custom subgroup. Delivery tracking and reply threading included.

Photo & video gallery

Drag‑drop uploads, event‑based albums, captioning, and one‑click sharing — without surrendering rights to a social platform.

Forms & documents

Permission slips, medical forms, bylaws, handbooks. Versioned, searchable, and gated to roles.

Money & dues

Track Scout accounts, dues, fundraising credits, and event payments. Stripe‑powered checkout.

Sign‑ups & surveys

Collect headcount, lunch choices, gear assignments, or feedback in seconds. Results export to CSV.

Public site + private hub

One side faces the world (recruiting!), the other is a member‑only command center. Same login, both views.

Provisioning, automated

Five minutes from sign‑up to live site.

You answer a short questionnaire. We generate a complete website preloaded with your troop's branding, leadership, calendar, and starter content.

  1. 1

    Tell us about your unit

    Unit number, type (troop / pack / crew), charter org, council, district, meeting time. Takes about 90 seconds.

  2. 2

    We provision your site

    Our pipeline spins up your subdomain (troop567.compass.app), seeds the database, generates pages, and sends you a login link.

  3. 3

    Invite your members

    Bulk‑invite parents and Scouts via email or CSV. Optionally import your existing Scoutbook roster.

  4. 4

    You're live

    Customize colors, add a logo, post your first event. Your unit's website is on the open web within minutes.

FAQ

Common questions.

Can we move from TroopWebHost?

Yes. Export your data from TroopWebHost (members, calendar, documents) and our import wizard will map it onto your new Compass site.

Does Compass work for Cub packs and Venturing crews?

Yes. Pick your unit type during sign‑up and the site is configured with the right ranks, awards, and structure.

Who owns our data?

You do. You can export everything at any time as JSON, CSV, or PDFs, and you can request full account deletion at any time.

Is Compass officially affiliated with Scouting America?

No. Compass is an independent product. We integrate with Scoutbook and Internet Advancement, but we are not endorsed by or affiliated with Scouting America.

How is youth privacy handled?

Per‑member privacy flags, role‑gated visibility, parental consent for minors, and Two‑Deep digital communication patterns are built in.

Can we use our own domain?

Yes — on every plan, including Patrol (free).
