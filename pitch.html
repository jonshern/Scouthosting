The problem

Volunteers run scouting on 2008-era software.

Most Scouts BSA troops, Cub Scout packs, and Girl Scout troops still publish on TroopWebHost, ScoutLander, or a frozen Squarespace from 2014. The parent group-text lives in GroupMe (which Microsoft just sunsetted). The newsletter goes out from someone's personal MailChimp. The campout RSVP is an Evite that bounced.

Every leader rebuilds the same broken stack on volunteer time, the recruitable-family sees a hit counter on the homepage, and the unit loses Scouts.

What Compass is

Three pillars: communication, organization, and security.

01 · Communication

Group chat with hard-coded YPT two-deep enforcement, the Sunday newsletter families actually open, and email + SMS broadcasts from the roster. Replaces: GroupMe, MailChimp, the parent-group-text-of-doom.

02 · Organization

Calendar with RSVPs and Stripe-collected dues, sign-up sheets for drivers and potlucks, trip plans with permission-slip tracking. Replaces: Evite, SignUpGenius, the spreadsheet on the committee chair's laptop.

03 · Security

YPT two-deep enforced in the schema (a misconfigured channel literally can't message youth). AES-256 at rest, SAML / OIDC / WebAuthn SSO, signed unsubscribe + RSVP tokens, append-only per-leader audit log. Replaces: nothing — most platforms don't bother.

Volunteer-led organizations are stickier than B2B SaaS — switching costs are paid in volunteer hours, not dollars. The cohort that signs up survives charter renewals (every May) at >90%. CAC is content-driven (Field Notes editorial + pack-leader Facebook groups) at ~$60.

Why us

Two-deep enforcement is a moat, not a feature.

Every platform that wants to enter this market has to figure out how to handle YPT — Youth Protection Training, the BSA's two-deep adult requirement on every Scouting activity. Off-the-shelf chat tools (Slack, Teams, Discord, GroupMe) can't pass a council's risk-management review because they don't know what YPT is.

Compass enforces two-deep in the data model. A channel-membership row literally cannot exist in a youth-containing channel without two YPT-current adult leaders from different households. A leader's training expiring auto-suspends every channel they were the second adult on. The audit trail is the product.
