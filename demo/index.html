About our troop

Sample Troop 100 is a fictional Scouts BSA unit chartered to Example Charter Organization in Anytown, USA. The troop "began" in 2010 and is part of District 1 in the Sample Council. This page exists to show what a real troop's About section can look like on Scouthosting.

  • High adventure every year. Rotating between national high‑adventure bases and major treks.
  • Scouting belongs outside. At least one outdoor activity each month.
  • Scout‑led. Adults guide and plan; Scouts run meetings and lead activities.
  • Service first. Regular community service projects with local partners.

What to expect at your first meeting

  1. Arrive a few minutes before 7:00 PM. Wear comfortable clothes.
  2. A Scout will greet you and introduce you to a patrol.
  3. Opening flag ceremony, then patrol activities or skills.
  4. Closing around 8:30 PM. We'll hand you a welcome packet.
